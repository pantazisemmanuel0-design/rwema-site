Documentation

Everything from account creation to autonomous trading. The whole journey takes about 20 minutes and needs no technical knowledge.

1. Create your account

Install the RWEMA Android app, sign up with your email, and confirm the verification message. The app is your control centre — status, trades, journal, performance, alerts, devices and settings all live there.

2. Subscribe

In the app: Settings → Subscribe. Choose Monthly, Annual, or the one-time Lifetime plan. Secure checkout is handled by Paddle, our merchant of record; your licence activates automatically the moment payment clears. If a renewal payment fails, the bot enters a 7-day grace period — it keeps managing open positions safely but opens nothing new.

3. Connect a VPS

You rent a Windows VPS from any provider (Hetzner, Contabo and DigitalOcean all work well). In the app, Settings → VPS & Devices gives you the installer download and a one-time connection token. Download rwema-install.exe onto the VPS, run it, and paste the token plus your MT5 server, login and password. The installer sets up MetaTrader 5, the trading engine, the supervising agent, Windows services and a credential vault — then verifies everything and starts in observe-only mode. No Python, no config files, no command line.

4. Connect MetaTrader 5

Your MT5 credentials are entered once, into the installer, and are encrypted on the VPS with Windows DPAPI — they are never transmitted to us. The bot connects to your account and begins scanning.

5. Start trading

Installation always finishes in observe-only mode. From the app you can start the bot and, when ready, enable live trading — which only activates for validated symbol and setup-grade populations. Every trade is deterministic and fully journaled with its reasoning.

6. Day-to-day

  • Dashboard — bot status, agent health, MT5 connection, equity.
  • Emergency Stop — flattens all positions within about 15 seconds, from anywhere.
  • Restart Agent — recycles the VPS supervisor without reinstalling.
  • Updates — signed packages are verified and applied automatically, with rollback on failure.
  • Recovery — VPS reboots, MT5 crashes and network loss all recover unattended.
