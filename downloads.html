Downloads

Downloads are tied to your subscription. Sign in inside the RWEMA app (Settings → Downloads) to get your personal, license-verified download links for:

  • RWEMA_AI_BOT_SETUP.exe — the Windows VPS installer (installs MT5, the trading engine, the agent, and all services in one run).
  • RWEMA_AI_BOT.apk — the Android app (also how new customers get started: install it, register, subscribe).

New customer?

Start with the Android app: choose a plan, and the confirmation email walks you through installing the app and connecting your VPS. The full journey takes about 20 minutes and needs no technical knowledge — the Customer Guide covers every step with screenshots.

System requirements

  • Windows Server 2019+ / Windows 10+ VPS, 2 GB RAM, 20 GB disk
  • An MT5 account with your broker (demo or live)
  • Android 8.0+ for the mobile app
