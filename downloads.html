Get started

Three steps, about 20 minutes. No coding, no config files.

⬇ Download the Android app RWEMA_AI_BOT.apk · 53 MB · Android 8.0+

Your phone may ask you to allow installs from your browser — that's normal for apps outside the Play Store.

  1. 1

    Install the app & create your account

    Open the app, sign up with your email, and confirm the verification message we send you.

  2. 2

    Choose your plan

    In the app: Settings → Subscribe. Secure checkout by Paddle. Your license activates the moment payment clears — nothing to type in.

    See plans and pricing →
  3. 3

    Connect your VPS

    In the app: Settings → VPS & Devices gives you the Windows installer link and a one-time connect token. Run it on your VPS, paste the token and your MT5 login — it installs everything and starts in observe-only mode.

What you'll need

  • A Windows VPS — Server 2019+ or Windows 10+, 2 GB RAM, 20 GB disk (Hetzner, Contabo and DigitalOcean all work well)
  • An MT5 account with your broker — demo or live
  • Android 8.0 or newer for the app

The Windows installer is issued through your account after you subscribe, so it always matches your licence and current version.

Stuck anywhere? We'll help — usually same day.
