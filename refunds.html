Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20

14-day refund window

If RWEMA AI BOT PRO isn't right for you, request a refund within 14 days of your first purchase and we will refund it in full. Email support@rwema.store from the address on your account — no justification required.

How refunds are issued

Payments are processed by Paddle.com, our merchant of record. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method by Paddle, normally within 5–10 business days depending on your bank. You can also raise a refund request directly with Paddle from any invoice email you receive.

Cancelling a subscription

You can cancel at any time from the billing portal linked in your invoice emails, or by emailing support. Cancellation stops future charges; your access continues until the end of the period you have already paid for. We do not pro-rate partial periods outside the 14-day window.

Renewals

Monthly and annual plans renew automatically until cancelled. Paddle emails you an invoice for every payment. If a renewal payment fails, your bot enters a 7-day grace period in which it manages existing positions safely but opens no new trades.

Lifetime purchases

The lifetime plan is a single payment and never renews. The same 14-day refund window applies from the date of purchase.

What we cannot refund

Trading losses are not refundable. RWEMA AI BOT PRO is software you operate on your own VPS with your own broker account; it does not guarantee profit, and market outcomes are outside our control. Please read the Terms of Service and risk disclosure before purchasing.

Contact

Refunds, billing questions, or anything else: support@rwema.store. We reply within one business day.
