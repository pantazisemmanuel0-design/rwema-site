Frequently asked questions

Does AI decide my trades?

No — never. Every BUY and SELL comes from deterministic Smart Money Concept rules. The same market data always produces the same decision, and each trade is journaled with its full reasoning. AI is used only for plain-language summaries, never to open or close a position.

Do you touch my money or my broker account?

No. You run the software on your own Windows server, connected to your own MetaTrader 5 account at your own broker, with your own credentials — which are encrypted on your machine and never sent to us. We never take custody of funds.

What do I actually need?

A Windows VPS (Server 2019+ / Windows 10+, 2 GB RAM), an MT5 account with your broker (demo or live), and an Android 8.0+ phone for the app.

How do I install it?

Install the Android app, subscribe, then open Settings → VPS & Devices to get the Windows installer and a one-time connection token. Run the installer on your VPS, paste the token and your MT5 login — it sets up everything and starts in observe-only mode. No config files, no command line.

What happens if my subscription lapses?

The bot stops opening new trades but keeps managing open positions to a safe close — it never abandons a funded account. Renewing reactivates everything automatically; your licence key never changes. There is a 7-day grace period so a failed card retry never interrupts you.

What if my VPS reboots or MT5 crashes?

A watchdog service restarts everything automatically and reconciles open positions from the journal, so management continues with no duplicates.

Can I move to a new VPS?

Yes — retire the old device in the app, then mint a token for the new one.

Which brokers and symbols are supported?

Any MetaTrader 5 broker. The software automatically resolves broker-specific symbol names (for example XAUUSD, XAUUSD.ecn or XAUUSDm). Live trading is enabled per validated symbol population; everything else runs in observe mode.
