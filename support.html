Support

Email support@rwema.store from the address on your account. If it's a VPS or install issue, include the install log path shown by the installer. We reply within one business day.

Frequently asked

Does AI place my trades?

No — never. Every trade decision comes from deterministic Smart Money Concept rules. The same market data always produces the same decision, and each trade's full reasoning is written to your journal.

What happens if my subscription lapses?

The bot stops opening new trades but keeps managing open positions to a safe close — it never abandons a funded account mid-trade. Renewing reactivates everything automatically; your license key never changes.

What if my VPS reboots?

The watchdog service restarts everything automatically, positions are reconciled from the journal, and management (break-even, trailing) continues. You don't need to do anything.

Can I move to a new VPS?

Yes — in the app: Settings → VPS & Devices → retire the old device, then mint a token and run the installer on the new one.

Emergency stop?

The red button on the app dashboard flattens every position and stops the bot within about 15 seconds, even if you're nowhere near the VPS.
