Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-19

What we collect

  • Account data — your email address and display name, held by our identity provider (Supabase).
  • Billing data — handled entirely by Paddle, our merchant of record. We never see or store your card details.
  • Operational data — bot status, trade history, journal entries, health telemetry, and device (VPS) metadata, used to run the service and show them to you in the app.

What we deliberately do NOT collect

  • Your MT5 password never leaves your VPS. It is encrypted there with Windows DPAPI and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • We store no card numbers and no government identity documents.

How data is protected

All transport is TLS. Agent credentials are stored only as SHA-256 hashes. Every consequential account action is written to an audit log. Access to production systems is restricted to the operator.

Data retention & deletion

Operational data is retained while your account is active. To delete your account and its data, email support@rwema.store from your account address; deletion completes within 30 days except where billing records must be kept for tax law.

Third parties

Supabase (authentication & database), Paddle (payments & invoices), Resend (transactional email), and your VPS provider (which you choose and contract with directly).
